About

Hello! My name is Senator Horton. Yes, Senator is my actual first name. My dad thought it would be funny and help me stand out. Well, it certainly helps with reservations at restaurants and getting reserved parking spaces, so mission accomplished, I guess.

This site is a place where I can share my thoughts and ideas with the world, as well as serve as a portfolio of sorts. I'm passionate about music, programming, web development, and game design, so you'll see a lot of that here.

Thanks for visiting my site! I hope you enjoy exploring my projects and thoughts as much as I enjoy sharing them.

Interests

  • Shredding on my guitar: Music is a huge part of my life, and I love expressing myself through my guitar.
  • DMing D&D games: I've been running the same campaign for my friends for three years now, and it's an amazing creative outlet.
  • Warhammer 40k: I'm a big fan of this expansive and strategic tabletop game.
  • Playing and making video games: Video games are both a hobby and a passion of mine, whether I'm playing or creating them.
  • App development: I enjoy creating useful and innovative applications.
  • Sating my hunger for knowledge: I'm always eager to learn new things and expand my horizons.
